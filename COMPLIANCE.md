# Draftpaper-loop Compliance

Draftpaper-loop is source-available for non-commercial research, education,
evaluation, and personal paper-writing workflows.

Commercial use requires separate prior written authorization from the project
developer. Commercial use includes SaaS deployment, hosted APIs, enterprise or
internal company deployment, paid manuscript-writing services, consulting
delivery, resale, sublicensing, bundling with paid products or courses, and
integration into commercial products.

Sponsorship or donation supports project maintenance but does not grant
commercial use rights. Commercial use requires separate prior written
authorization.

The public project uses visible, auditable, non-executing provenance and schema
metadata. It does not use hidden executable payloads, invisible watermarks,
telemetry, device fingerprinting, remote license checks, destructive
anti-removal behavior, or malware-like tricks.
